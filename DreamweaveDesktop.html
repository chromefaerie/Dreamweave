DREAMWEAVE STUDIO

Welcome to Dreamweave Studio. I'm SONJA SCHOUTEN, a multidisciplinary artist and designer with focuses on creative direction, digital design, pixel art, and branding.

Working in video, photo, graphic, and video game mediums, I love to infuse nostalgia and play into my projects.

Tell me all about your project dreams! I work with a team of highly skilled collaborators to weave your dreams into reality.
